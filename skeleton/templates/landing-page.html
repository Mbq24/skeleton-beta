WHAT IS SKELETON?
Skeleton is a decentralized banking protocol that offers 0% interest on collateralized loans, borrowing up to 90% of the Loan-To-Value ratio.

Users can borrow against digital assets or use existing lines of credit to borrow on-chain and autonomously transact with newly issued capital off-chain.

Skeleton operates as a direct solution to the issues of high fees, interest rates and private ledgers found in centralized banking. This platform enables users to seamlessly bank at the user’s discretion, while preserving wealth and gaining immediate access to capital for real-world spending in a peer-to-peer, trustful manner.

Q: Is Skeleton a bank?  A: Currently, Skeleton acts as a DLT neobank, but our roadmap includes our eventual bank independence through Free-Zone banking and processing.

 

Q: Is Skeleton Decentralized? A: FThe DAG network on which our protocol and pools are built is fully decentralized, but our off-chain payments ledger is centralized to interact with the banking system and card processing networks for consolidation - this is an unavoidable reality.

 

Q: Does Skeleton have governance?

A: No. The native SKEL token is governance free, as there is no Skeleton governance.

 

Q: Do I have to take out a loan to spend with the Skeleton Card? A: No. You can connect your current wallets, cards or accounts and start spending today.

 

Q: What assets does Skeleton support?

A: We currently accept USD (fiat), USDC, PAXG and DAG for collateral.
